Did you know?

Welcome to facts about octopuses!The wonderfully weird octopus may be as close to alien life as we can get today.

Fact 1

1. Octopus means eight feet in Greek.

Fact 2

2. Over 300 species of octopus roam every ocean on Earth

Fact 3

3. They did not always have eight arms. In 2022, researchers published a study on a vampyropod fossil with ten functional limbs.

Fact 4

4. Octopus blood is blue. It can transport oxygen more efficiently in cold environments with low oxygen levels

Fact 5

5. Octopus arms are super sensors. These can detect chemicals, essentially tasting and smelling whatever they touch.
